Balls 1–20 · Slow swing drill
  • 1
    First 5 balls at 50% effort. So slow you can physically feel the clubface through impact. You are learning the sensation — not trying to hit it anywhere.
  • 2
    The one feeling: forearms rotating left through impact — like turning a door handle to the left as you swing through. This is the physical action that closes the face.
  • 3
    Gradually build to 65% across the next 15 balls. Only add speed when the face feels like it's closing. If it opens again — drop back to 50%.
Balls 21–40 · Build up
  • 4
    70% maximum from here. Setup checklist, one practice swing, commit within 10 seconds.
  • 5
    One swing thought only: forearms rotate, swing right. Nothing else.
  • 6
    Bad shot — step back. Recheck tee height and grip. Those two cause 80% of open face shots. Never rush the next ball after a bad one.
  • 7
    Good shot — note the feeling immediately. Replicate it on the very next ball while it's fresh.
